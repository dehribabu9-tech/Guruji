The Nutana Unnata Abhilasha (NUA) Odisha scheme is a flagship state initiative designed to empower youth through continuous skilling, reskilling, and upskilling. Launched by the Government of Odisha with an outlay of ₹385 crore, the program aims to skill at least one lakh youth annually over three years (FY 2023–24 to 2025–26).
